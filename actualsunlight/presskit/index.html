Description

Actual Sunlight is a short interactive story about love, depression and the corporation.

Widely considered to be one of the bleakest and best-written experiences in indie gaming, Actual Sunlight challenges you to confront the life of Evan Winter: An overweight, lonely and severely depressed young professional.

History

First released in January 2013 for Windows, Actual Sunlight was subsequently released on the PlayStation Vita in August 2015, and then on iOS and Android in November 2017.

Actual Sunlight

A short interactive story about love, depression and the corporation.

About the Game

Actual Sunlight is a linear interactive fiction experience. The game is available via Steam and itch.io for Windows and Mac. Mobile versions are also available for iOS and Android, as well as the PlayStation Vita.

The game is between two and three hours in length, with a script of approximately fifteen-thousand words. It was built RPG Maker VX ACE, and later ported to both Unity 5 and RPG Maker MV.

I developed this game personally and released it via my own studio, WZO Games Inc.

Intense, Memorable Writing

Dealing with subjects such as depression and suicide requires a deft touch, and the ability to delve deep into a character that may be painful or uneasy for an audience to identify with.

Actual Sunlight has been widely recognized for doing this in a way that is engaging - humourous at times, even - while also resonating as deeply genuine.

Working Within Limits

As my first game, I wandered into the creation of Actual Sunlight with no development experience or mentoring whatesover, and used nothing but a copy of RPG Maker VX ACE to make the initial free version of the game.

On the strength of that, I was able to complete a small crowdfunding campaign to add original art and music to the game, ultimately beginning a journey that led to a year-long wait on Steam Greenlight before approval and a wider distribution.

Moving to Consoles

After a successful year on Steam, I worked with Sony to secure a development kit and completed a PS Vita version of the game in Unity 5. The game was well-received by the console audience, and Actual Sunlight was ultimately selected as a PS+ title.

Throughout this entire journey, the game continued to be buoyed by its powerful writing and narrative, with many commenting that it simply had to be played in order to be understood.

Actual Sunlight has created many positive and hard-hitting experiences, and many have written about it accordingly.

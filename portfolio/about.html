About Me

Born and raised in Toronto, enthusiastic gamer, lifelong writer and friend of animals.

Super Games Man

I get it: A brief glance at my work - as well as learning that I was on the IGF Narrative Jury, was the Festival Director for Wordplay, etc. - may give you the idea that I only exist in a tiny bubble of uppity indie intelligentsia.

But it's not true! I don't just sit around playing nothing but Twine games - I have tons of time in Destiny (and both of the achievement t-shirts!) as well as hundreds of hours in games like DOTA 2, Enter the Gungeon, WoW, and more.

I have an honest and earnest love for games of all stripes, and I enjoy seeing games possess the narratives which make them the best they can be.

Education & Other Work

I graduated from the University of Toronto with a double major in English and Philosophy. I then completed a one-year postgrad certificate at Centennial College in Corporate Communications and Public Relations.

From there, I went on to work as a web specialist, copywriter and creative director at a variety of companies and agencies, eventually specializing in large-scale corporate events for the financial and pharmaceutical sectors.

For a comprehensive history of my corporate career, I would suggest you take a look at my LinkedIn Profile.

Personal Interests

I like animals a lot. I wrote a humourous book called Animals Who Need Big Kisses, and I enjoy going to places like the Toronto Zoo, as well as to visit friends who have pets.

Outside of the games I've shipped, my proudest accomplishment is being the holder of a FitBit 'Olympian Sandal' badge for hiking 100,000 steps in a single day. That was a long day.

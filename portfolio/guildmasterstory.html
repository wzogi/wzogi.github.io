Guildmaster Story

A puzzle-match comedy about fame, misfortune and working for nothing.

About the Game

Guildmaster Story is a puzzle-match game with a visual novel foundation. The game is available for iOS and Android.

The game is between one and two hours, with new content scheduled for release throughout 2019. It was built in Unity 2018 with the Puzzle Match Kit template by GameVanilla and the Fungus narrative extension by Snozbot.

I wrote and developed this game personally and released it via my own studio, WZO Games Inc.

Experience Points and Unpaid Labour

The fundamental narrative idea in Guildmaster Story is found in how it utilizes the RPG-based concept of 'Experience Points' as a metaphor for the 'valuable experience' and 'exposure' that many people today receive for their work in lieu of traditional monetary compensation, particularly in professions that are viewed as being intrinsically prestigious.

A Mobile Game Within A Mobile Game

The story also playfully makes fun of mobile games in a way that allows for an effective explanation of why the puzzle-match gameplay exists in the first place: When solving puzzles, the player is literally playing from the perspective of the main character of the story who is foolishly ignoring and procrastinating his way through life.

This aspect of the game is also given a further twist when the player learns that they are not inhabiting the the main character, but rather that they themselves are his phone, a joke which then continues both within the game and in the embedded iOS / Android notifications.

Making the Asset Store Sing

With the exception of character art by the talented Bessie Yu, every other creative asset (beyond the writing, of course) was sourced from the Unity Asset Store, and other third-party asset marketplaces. I undertook a substantial amount of effort and creative direction to weave these into a cohesive and sharp-looking whole that gives and overall impression of polish and harmony.

Press & Reception

Guildmaster Story has gathered a wide variety of positive impressions from fans and critics alike. The game has overwhelmingly positive scores on the App Store and Google Play, and has been reviewed and featured in major gaming publications.

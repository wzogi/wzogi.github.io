About Me

More information about my personal and professional background.

My Unique Strengths

In addition to being a proven writer and developer of narrative-focused games, I also have a strong corporate communications and agency writing background which allows me to integrate easily and efficiently into professional creative environments.

I'm experienced in working quickly, adjusting on the fly to meet the needs of other aspects of a production, and I am as comfortable working within the framework of an existing or collaborative idea as I am in iterating upon my own.

My Skills

I've worked with a wide variety of writing tools, including Microsoft Word, Final Draft, Montage, Articy, and more.

I know my way around things like Photoshop, Final Cut and various audio DAWs well enough to survive, can code HTML / CSS, am Unity Certified, and for a career writer I have a fairly high level of aptitude for all things computer-esque.

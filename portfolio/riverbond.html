Riverbond

Embark down an enchanted river, rebuilding a passage to The Great Beyond.

About the Game

Riverbond is an action-adventure game by Cococucumber with a heroic narrative for 1-4 player local co-op set in a charming voxel world.

The game is currently in development, with more details to come in 2018. Similar to Planet of the Eyes, I am working with Cococucumber as the sole narrative designer and writer on the project.

About the Game

SUPERLIMINAL is a puzzle game by Pillow Castle Games. By using the ambiguity of depth, this first-person puzzle game allows players to explore a surreal world by manipulating objects in physical space according to their perspective.

The game is currently in development, and was released in 2019 on the Epic Game Store, with later releases following on all major consoles. I am the sole scriptwriter on the game, and am also handling direction of voiceover. For an additional sampling of my work in both those roles, please check out our most recent trailer.

A narrative-focused stock market simulator. Get rich or cry trying.

About the Game

The Invisible Hand is a stock market simluator by Power Struggle Games. With a real simulation at its core, the player lives in the shoes of a newly-hired trader at the ethically and legally dubious Ferios Capital.

The game was released in 2021 on the Steam, with additional releases on Humble and GOG as well. I was the sole scriptwriter on the game, collaborating with the team at Power Struggle on the characters and overall story.
